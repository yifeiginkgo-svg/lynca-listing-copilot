# Pokemon Examples

Pokemon titles should preserve market rarity terms.

Important terms:

- SAR
- AR
- SR
- SIR
- EX
- GX
- V
- VMAX
- VSTAR
- Master Ball
- Pokeball Reverse
- set name
- card number
- grade

Good:

`Pokemon Japanese 151 Charizard ex SAR 201/165 PSA 10`

Bad:

`Rare Pokemon Charizard Card`

Rules:

- Pokemon name drives the title.
- Preserve language or regional product if visible or known.
- Preserve card number when visible.
- Do not invent rarity if the symbol or text is not visible.
