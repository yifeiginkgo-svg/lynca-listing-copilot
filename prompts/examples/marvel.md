# Marvel Examples

Marvel titles should preserve character and parallel terminology.

Important terms:

- PMG
- Precious Metal Gems
- Seismic Gold
- Red PMG
- Green PMG
- Jambalaya
- Platinum Portraits
- sketch
- artist
- serial number
- grade

Good:

`2023 Marvel Platinum Spider-Man Seismic Gold Refractor /10`

Good:

`2013 Fleer Retro Marvel Wolverine Red PMG Precious Metal Gems /100`

Bad:

`Rare Marvel Superhero Card`

Rules:

- Character comes first unless product convention strongly favors year first.
- Preserve exact parallel terminology.
- Do not collapse PMG into generic `parallel`.
